FAQ

Common questions about the MVP direction

Why launch only in London first?

−
London gives the platform a focused launch market with strong local service demand. It reduces complexity, makes operations easier and helps validate the model before expanding into other cities.

Why not start as a broad marketplace?

+
A narrower service-led niche is easier to position, easier to market and less likely to compete directly with very large horizontal platforms.

Can this expand to other cities later?

+
Yes. The data structure, category logic, filters and search flow are already designed in a way that can scale city by city.

How does monetisation work at launch?

+
The easiest launch options are featured listings and lead-generation actions such as calls, quotes and messages. Google Ads placements can be added as traffic grows.
